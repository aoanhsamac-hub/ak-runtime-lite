# Alkasik Kingdom Budget Law v1.0 FINAL

**Date:** 2026-06-08
**Status:** FINAL
**Authority:** Hung Vuong
**Reviewer:** Sage
**Owner Agent:** Janus

---

## 1. Revenue Sources

Kingdom Revenue shall be derived from the following approved sources:

| Source | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Trading Revenue | Profits from market trading operations |
| Investment Revenue | Returns on capital investments |
| Service Revenue | Income from services rendered |
| Technology Revenue | Income from technology products |
| Licensing Revenue | Income from intellectual property licensing |
| Consulting Revenue | Income from consulting engagements |
| Education Revenue | Income from educational programs |
| Content Revenue | Income from content and media |
| E-Commerce Revenue | Income from commerce operations |
| Infrastructure Services | Income from infrastructure services |
| Other Approved Sources | Any source approved by Hung Vuong |

---

## 2. Revenue Allocation Model

### Mandatory Split

```
Kingdom Revenue
├── Kingdom Treasury: 92%
└── Royal Treasury: 8%
```

This split is mandatory for all revenue sources unless specifically exempted by Hung Vuong.

## 4. Budget Cycle

### Monthly Cycle
1. Iris prepares monthly budget proposal
2. Sage reviews for risk compliance
3. Janus coordinates and consolidates
4. Hung Vuong approves

### Quarterly Cycle
1. Budget execution review
2. Surplus/deficit analysis
3. Reserve rebalancing
4. Next quarter allocation adjustment

### Annual Cycle
1. Full annual budget proposal
2. Strategic priority review
3. Revenue projection update
4. Reserve target setting
5. Hung Vuong final approval

---

## 5. Budget Approval Authority

| Step | Role | Authority |
|------|------|-----------|
| Proposal | Iris | Prepare budget with revenue projections |
| Risk Review | Sage | Assess risk, verify compliance |
| Coordination | Janus | Consolidate, resolve conflicts, present |
| Final Approval | Hung Vuong | Approve or return for revision |

Emergency budgets require: Iris → Sage → Janus → Hung Vuong (expedited)

---

## 6. Strategic Reserve

### Rules
- Minimum balance: 3 months of operating budget
- Funding: 10% of monthly Kingdom Treasury allocation
- Usage: Only with Sage + Janus + Hung Vuong approval
- Replenishment: First priority after any drawdown

### Flow
```
Revenue → Reserve Allocation (10%) → Operating Budget → Development Budget
```

---

## 7. Emergency Reserve

### Rules
- Minimum balance: 1 month of operating budget
- Funding: 5% of monthly Kingdom Treasury allocation until target met
- Usage: Only for defined emergency events (see AK_EMERGENCY_RESERVE_FRAMEWORK)
- Approval: Agent → Janus → Hung Vuong

---

## 8. Surplus Distribution

After all budget obligations are met:

1. Janus certifies surplus amount
2. Sage verifies calculation
3. Up to 50% of surplus may be distributed to Royal Treasury
4. Remaining surplus retained in Kingdom Treasury
5. Hung Vuong authorizes final distribution

---

## 9. Compliance

- All expenditures must have approved budget line item
- Budget amendments require same approval chain as original
- Monthly budget execution reports to Janus
- Quarterly reports to Hung Vuong
- Annual audit by Sage-appointed reviewer
