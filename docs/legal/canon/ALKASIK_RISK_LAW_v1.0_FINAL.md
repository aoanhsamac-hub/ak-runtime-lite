## Key Requirements (from available documentation)

### Risk Classification

| Level | Label | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| LEVEL_0_LOW | Low | Standard change, no material risk |
| LEVEL_1_MODERATE | Moderate | Some risk, requires agent review |
| LEVEL_2_HIGH | High | Significant risk, requires Sage review |
| LEVEL_3_CRITICAL | Critical | National risk, requires Janus + Sage |
| LEVEL_4_CONSTITUTIONAL | Constitutional | Constitutional risk, requires Hung Vuong |

### Risk Governance

- All changes must be classified before execution
- Risk kernel modifications require LEVEL_4_CONSTITUTIONAL
- No autonomous risk escalation by agents
- Sage is the sole risk review authority
- Risk kernel must be protected from direct agent modification

### Prohibited Actions

- Agents may not reclassify their own risk level
- Agents may not bypass risk review
- Agents may not modify risk kernel without Sage + Hung Vuong
- No execution without completed risk assessment

### Mandatory Review Requirements

| Risk Level | Required Reviewers | Required Approver |
|---|---|---|
| LEVEL_0_LOW | None | None |
| LEVEL_1_MODERATE | Agent Lead | Agent Lead |
| LEVEL_2_HIGH | Sage | Sage |
| LEVEL_3_CRITICAL | Sage | Janus |
| LEVEL_4_CONSTITUTIONAL | Sage + Council | Hung Vuong |
