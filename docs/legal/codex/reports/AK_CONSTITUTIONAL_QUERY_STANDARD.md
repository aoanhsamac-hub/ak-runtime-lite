Version: v1.0
Status: ACTIVE
Authority: Hung Vuong
Owner: Lang Lieu
Reviewer: Sage

## Mandatory Query Structure

Every technical directive MUST include:

### AUTHORITY
Legal authority reference from AK-CODEX.

### LEGAL BASIS
Specific Constitution, Law, Policy or Standard invoked.

### SCOPE
Legal/governance scope only.

### DELIVERABLES
All legal artifact outputs required.

### COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST
Laws verified against checklist.

### EXIT CRITERIA
PASS/FAIL conditions.

### STOP CONDITIONS
Conditions requiring immediate halt.

## Query Order

1. Constitution (if applicable)
2. State Corpus (if applicable)
3. Laws (by domain)
4. Policies (by authority)
5. Standards (by domain)

## Conflict Resolution

Higher authority supersedes.

Constitution > Law > Policy > Standard > Specification.
