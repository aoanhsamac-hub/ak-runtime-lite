## 1. Purpose

This charter establishes the legal foundation for kingdom treasury operations. It defines the authority, structure, responsibilities, and governance of the Alkasik Kingdom Treasury.

## 4. Responsibilities

### Kingdom Treasury
- Manage 92% of national revenue
- Allocate budget to kingdom programs
- Maintain strategic and emergency reserves
- Report financial status to Hung Vuong
- Ensure compliance with Economic Law

### Treasury Operator (Iris)
- Monitor revenue flows
- Prepare budget proposals
- Conduct economic assessments
- Track treasury impact of capability activations
- Report to Janus on treasury status

### Governance Reviewer (Sage)
- Review budget proposals for risk
- Audit treasury operations
- Verify compliance with allocation model
- Flag governance violations

---

## 6. Reporting Requirements

- **Monthly**: Treasury status report to Janus
- **Quarterly**: Budget execution report to Hung Vuong
- **Annual**: Full financial audit report
- **Event-driven**: Treasury impact assessment for any PILOT_ACTIVE+ activation

---

## 7. Audit Requirements

- All treasury transactions must be auditable
- Audit log must be append-only
- Sage must review quarterly treasury reports
- Annual external audit by Hung Vuong-appointed reviewer
- No treasury record may be deleted (per Retention Decree)

---

## 8. Compliance Requirements

- All budget allocations must follow the 92/8 split
- No expenditure without approved budget line item
- Emergency withdrawals require Sage + Janus + Hung Vuong approval
- Strategic reserve must maintain minimum balance
- Digital asset operations are RESERVED — not active
