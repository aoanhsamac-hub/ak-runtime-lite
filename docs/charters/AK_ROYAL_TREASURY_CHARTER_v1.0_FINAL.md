# Alkasik Kingdom Royal Treasury Charter v1.0 FINAL

**Date:** 2026-06-08
**Status:** FINAL
**Authority:** Hung Vuong
**Owner:** Hung Vuong
**Reviewer:** Sage

---

## 1. Purpose

This charter defines the governance, authority, and operational boundaries of the Royal Treasury of Alkasik Kingdom. The Royal Treasury is the sovereign financial arm under direct authority of Hung Vuong.

---

## 2. Ownership

| Field | Value |

| Sovereign Owner | Hung Vuong |

| Kingdom Treasury | Janus (on behalf of Hung Vuong) |
| Legal Basis | Economic Law v1.0 FINAL |

---

## 3. Revenue Allocation

### Approved Model

```
Kingdom Revenue
├── Kingdom Treasury: 92%
└── Royal Treasury: 8%
```

### Allocation Rights

Hung Vuong has sole authority over:
- Royal Treasury 8% revenue allocation
- Surplus distribution from Kingdom Treasury to Royal Treasury
- Capital contribution rights from Royal Treasury to Kingdom Treasury
- Emergency support from Royal Treasury to Kingdom Treasury

---

## 6. Revenue Allocation Rights

Hung Vuong may:
- Direct Royal Treasury funds to any lawful purpose
- Delegate allocation authority to Janus for defined operations
- Adjust allocation percentages (requires constitutional amendment if changing 92/8 split)

Hung Vuong may NOT:
- Commingle Royal Treasury funds with Kingdom Treasury
- Use Royal Treasury to bypass Kingdom Treasury governance
- Allocate to unlawful or unconstitutional purposes

---

## 7. Surplus Distribution Rights

When Kingdom Treasury generates surplus after all program allocations:

1. Janus certifies surplus amount
2. Sage verifies surplus calculation
3. Hung Vuong authorizes distribution
4. Up to 50% of surplus may flow to Royal Treasury
5. Remaining surplus stays in Kingdom Treasury as reserve

---

## 8. Audit Boundaries

| Scope | Auditor | Frequency |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Royal Treasury transactions | Hung Vuong-appointed auditor | Annual |
| Royal Treasury compliance | Sage | Quarterly |
| Separation compliance | Sage | Annual |
| Kingdom Treasury → Royal Treasury flows | Sage | Per transaction |

Royal Treasury audit reports are confidential to Hung Vuong.

---

## 9. Separation from Kingdom Treasury

The Royal Treasury and Kingdom Treasury are legally separate entities:

| Domain | Kingdom Treasury | Royal Treasury |
|--------|------------------|----------------|
| Owner | Janus | Hung Vuong |
| Revenue share | 92% | 8% |
| Budget governance | Iris → Janus → Sage → Hung Vuong | Hung Vuong only |
| Audit | Sage review, public | Hung Vuong-appointed, confidential |
| Reserve | Strategic + Emergency | Capital + Sovereign |
| Surplus | Distribution to Royal Treasury (up to 50%) | Retained |
