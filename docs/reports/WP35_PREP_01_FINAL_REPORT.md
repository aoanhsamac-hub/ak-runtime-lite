## Key Design Decisions

1. **Governance-first architecture** — Every learning transition requires review gate passage
2. **7 registries** — Separate registries for each pipeline stage, not one monolithic store
3. **5 maturity levels** — Capabilities evolve through EMERGING to SOVEREIGN
4. **4-tier risk classification** — LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH/SOVEREIGN with escalating approval authority
5. **16-week implementation** — 8 work packages, each with defined review gates
6. **Permanent retention** for approved skills and capabilities
7. **Immutable records** with status transition history
